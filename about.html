LOS PEPES STUDIO

Los Pepes is an artistic duo (Meggie Prata and Francisco Leal) that create bold and euphoric artworks with the intention of spreading positive thoughts and feelings to everyone who sees it.

Also known as ‘Gangsters of Love’ their goals as artists follow along this line of exploring exactly what colours can do to the human mind. With their combination of spray painted street art murals and digitally rendered designs, colour is as much of a common motif in their work as the smiley face, the heart or a host of other fantastical interpretations of humans and animals.

Color is happiness, and that is Los Pepes’ favorite medium
